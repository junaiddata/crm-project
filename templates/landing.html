Features

Everything your sales team needs

From first enquiry to closed deal — manage the full lead lifecycle without switching tools.

Spreadsheet-style table

Inline cell editing — click any field to edit directly. No forms, no page reloads. As fast as a spreadsheet, backed by a database.

Kanban pipeline board

Visualise your entire pipeline by status. Switch between table and board view in one click. Drag status from dropdown on any card.

Follow-up tracking

Log two rounds of follow-up per lead — date and notes. Never lose track of a pending conversation or promised callback.

Quotation file upload

Attach a PDF or document to any lead. Files are stored on the server and linked back to the lead — no more hunting through email.

Live dashboard stats

Instant overview of total leads, approved count, conversion rate, pending follow-ups, and monthly intake — updated in real time.

Search, filter & export

Full-text search across all fields. Filter by salesperson, platform, or status. Export any view to CSV with one click.

WhatsApp inbox NEW

Incoming WhatsApp enquiries land automatically via the Meta Cloud API webhook. View, filter, and mark messages as replied from one inbox.
